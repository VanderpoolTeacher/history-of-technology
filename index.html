The History of Technology

A traditional 3-credit-hour, lower-division general-education college course surveying the history of technology from roughly 1750 to the present. The course treats technology not as a sequence of isolated inventions but as a social process — examining how machines, techniques, and systems both reshaped and were shaped by the societies that built them.
