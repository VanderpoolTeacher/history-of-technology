# The History of Technology

A traditional 3-credit-hour, lower-division general-education college course surveying the
history of technology from roughly 1750 to the present.

This repository is the public, learner-facing build of the course, published via **GitHub
Pages**. Open `index.html` (or the Pages site) to browse the lessons.

- 8 modules, 17 lessons (Pre-industrial background → AI & the present + a case-study capstone)
- Produced by Forge from the course's design artifacts. Instructor-only material
  (answer keys, instructor notes) is intentionally excluded from this public build.
